SOFTWARE DEVELOPERS

Premier Payments provides a secure payment processing solution that can be easily integrated into your point-of-sale (POS) systems.

When a developer utilizes our proprietary gateway, they leverage our secure payment platforms that feature tokenization, end to end encryption and SSL connection to ensure all your merchants data remains secure. As well as providing secure processing solutions, Premier Payments offers a lifetime residual program, and advanced online reporting tools to ensure 100% transparency.
