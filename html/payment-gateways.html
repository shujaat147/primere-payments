Premier Charge

Premier Payment Charge is our sister company and offers a powerful online gateway to help your business process transactions quickly, safely, and securely.

From our Merchant Console Virtual Terminal to hosted payment forms, their solutions allow you to operate your business with all the functions of a credit card machine. Tools such as ePay Charge which turn any computer into a credit card machine, and Premier Charge Mobile similarly turns mobile devices such as iPhone, Blackberrys and Windows Mobile phones into mobile credit card terminals.

Authorize.net

One of the industry’s leading payment gateways, Authorize.net manages the routing of transactions just like a traditional credit card swipe machine.

This meets the evolving needs of online and retail merchants by making it easy to accept payments from their e-commerce website. They provide the complex infrastructure and security necessary to ensure fast, reliable and secure transmission of transaction data.

BENEFITS

  • Accept all major credit cards, eCheck.Net (electronic checks from bank accounts), gift cards, and debit cards.

  • Full-featured Merchant Interface to monitor and control payments through your Web site.

  • Identify suspicious transactions with our value-adding products and built-in fraud tools.

  • Sensitive data is stored in the Authorize.Net PCI-compliant data center, never on your computer.

  • Your funds are automatically deposited into your merchant bank account within days.

  • Live technical and account support for merchants, as well as access to online documentation and user guides
